About Sean "DasSkippy" Frederico

I am a male American Computer Game Design Student studying at University of California Santa Cruz

I am a game designer and developer with a passion for creating engaging interactive experiences. I have built several projects in Unity and Minecraft, which have been played by over 25,000 players worldwide. I am experienced in C, C#, Unity, and Blender, and I enjoy combining technical skills with creativity to bring stories to life.

Watching players explore and enjoy my games has been one of the most rewarding parts of my journey, and it continues to inspire me to pursue a career in game design.
